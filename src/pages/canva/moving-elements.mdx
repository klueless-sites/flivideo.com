## Move elements one pixel at a time

- Click on the element you want to move
- Press `up`, `down`, `left`, `right` arrow keys to move

## Move elements 10 pixels at a time

- Click on the element you want to move
- Press `shift` + `up`, `down`, `left`, `right` arrow keys to move
