## Three techniques

There are different ways to change the design name.

1. Click the 'Edit' icon below the design thumbnail

Edit the design name

2. Click the '...' icon at top of the design thumbnail

Edit the design name

3. Click on the thumbnail to open design

Click on the design name in the top right area just to the left of your profile picture

Edit the design name
