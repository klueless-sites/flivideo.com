## Using the alignment controls

- Click on element
- Click on the `Position` menu
- Click on the type of alignment you want

## Using rulers

- Click on `File` > `View Settings` > `Show rulers and guides`
- Drag rulers horizontally or vertically to create guides
- Drag elements to align them with guides
