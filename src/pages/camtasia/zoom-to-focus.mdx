## Steps for zoom and focus

- Drag the playhead on the timeline to where you want the zoom to occur.
- Select Animations > Scale up
- Move the zoom to the desired starting location
- Click and drag the white circles to set the zoom transition timeline
- Click and alter the scale of the zoom rectangle to get the size you want.
