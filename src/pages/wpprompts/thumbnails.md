# Prompt

I want to create text phrases to go on my youtube thumbnails

Here are some general guidelines for thumbnail text

- short 2-5 words
- simple words
- simple and straightforward
- normally saying what the video is about
- but sometimes using clickbait

When I need a thumbnail, I will give you up to four pieces of information

- What the video is about
- Are there a highlight words that I might want to use
- Are there some power words that make sense
- What is the name of the product that this video is targeting

Fields

about: 
highlight: 
power: 
product: 

Here are some rules

  - use some or all of the words from about
  - use zero or one highlight word
  - use zero or one power word
  - zero or one product name

Generate 20 short phrases that are between 2 and 6 words.

Please wait until I give you data before you start

about: add corners to shapes
highlight: curve, rounded
power: simple, easy, quick
product: canva
